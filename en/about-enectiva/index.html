Enectiva is an energy management software solution designed for office buildings, business centres, and other buildings of commercial nature. Typically, it serves companies, building owners, investors, developers, and people engaged in facility management or property management, whom it helps in keeping their energy usage as effective as possible.

Main Benefits:

  • Keeps all energy under control at all times
  • Detects energy waste and motivates its saving
  • Simple comparison of occupants, buildings and complexes with one another according to consumption and costs
  • Automatic reports and emergency notices
  • Value added for tenants and residents

Licence Terms:

  • A licence applies to the particular number of buildings and meters
  • The number of users under a licence is always unlimited

Designed for:

  • Building owners
  • Companies engaged in building management
  • Property management companies
  • Asset management companies
  • Developers
  • Companies installing energy meters

Other Features:

Connection to EEX and PXE Energy Exchanges

With this feature you can keep track of the development at energy exchanges in Prague and in Germany. The application notifies you automatically of the most convenient time for energy purchase with regard to your portfolio.

Detailed Analyses of Savings and Cost-Saving Measures

Each event on your building is recorded in the application. If you invest with the intention to lower the energy costs of the building, not only does Enectiva monitor the consumption reduction, but it also calculates the return of the investment, cash-flow, and other economic parameters of your project.

Google Maps Support

The location of each building is accurately entered into the map. You are therefore constantly in touch with your portfolio, and you simply choose which building you want to analyze directly in the map.

Integration of Weather in the Location of the Building

Thanks to the connection to weather stations, you can compare buildings with one another with regard to the outside climatic conditions. Thus, you can find out easily the influence of temperature in a particular area on the heat or gas consumption.

Support of Remote and Mass Manual Meter Reading

Meter reading data are always the basis of energy management. Enectiva enables you to effectively enter mass readings of the entire portfolio, and to collect data from the automatic readings systems as well.
